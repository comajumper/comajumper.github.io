Dima Bykov is a design and product leader who empower people to bring visionary ideas into life with intention, simplicity and beauty.

Currently, as VP of Product at Osome, he is shaping the future of financial accounting for SMEs in Singapore, Hong Kong, and the UK.

Previously, he was leading design and research for ecommerce unit at Yandex called Russian Amazon by Financial Times.

He is a mentor at Meander and ADPList and was a programme director and lecturer at Yandex Design School.
